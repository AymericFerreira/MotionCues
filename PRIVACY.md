# Privacy Policy — Motion Cues

**Motion Cues does not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

- **No data collection.** The app does not gather analytics, usage statistics, or any personal
  information.
- **No network access.** The app makes no network requests. The only outbound action is opening a
  link (e.g. the Ko-fi support page or the source repository) in your browser when you tap it.
- **No tracking, no ads, no third-party SDKs.** There is no Firebase, no Google Play Services
  analytics, and no advertising.
- **Sensors stay on-device.** Accelerometer readings are used only, in real time, to animate the
  dot overlay and to detect vehicle motion. They are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
- **Settings stay on-device.** Your preferences are saved locally with Android DataStore and never
  leave your phone.

Because no data is collected, there is nothing to request, export, or delete beyond uninstalling the
app.

If you have questions, open an issue on the project's source repository.
